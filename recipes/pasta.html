Pasta Recipe

Description

Indulge in a culinary symphony of flavors with this Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta. Imagine strands of perfectly cooked pasta enveloped in a luxurious sauce that marries the bold essence of garlic with the rich creaminess of Parmesan cheese. As you take your first bite, the aroma of sautéed garlic dances on your palate, tantalizing your senses with its fragrant allure. Each forkful reveals a harmonious blend of textures, as the silky smoothness of the cream sauce coats every strand of pasta, ensuring a decadent experience with every mouthful.

Topped with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese, this dish is a celebration of comfort and sophistication, inviting you to savor each bite until the very last. Whether enjoyed as a comforting weeknight meal or showcased as the star of a dinner party, this Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta promises to delight even the most discerning of palates with its irresistible charm.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)
  • Additional grated Parmesan cheese for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pasta:

    • Cook your favorite pasta according to the package instructions until it reaches al dante texture. Drain and set aside.

  2. Sauté Garlic:

    • Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add 3-4 cloves of minced garlic to the skillet.
    • Sauté the garlic for about 1 minute until it becomes fragrant, being careful not to let it burn.

  3. Make the Cream Sauce:

    • Pour 1 cup of heavy cream into the skillet with the sautéed garlic.
    • Bring the cream to a simmer, stirring occasionally.
    • Let the cream cook for about 2-3 minutes until it slightly thickens.

  4. Add Parmesan Cheese:

    • Gradually sprinkle in 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese into the simmering cream sauce.
    • Stir continuously until the Parmesan cheese is fully melted and the sauce becomes smooth.

  5. Season the Sauce:

    • Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.

  6. Combine Pasta and Sauce:

    • Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce.
    • Toss the pasta in the sauce until it is evenly coated.

  7. Garnish and Serve:

    • Optional: Garnish the creamy pasta with chopped parsley for a fresh flavor.
    • Optional: Sprinkle additional grated Parmesan cheese on top for extra cheesiness.

  8. Serve the creamy garlic Parmesan pasta hot and enjoy!
