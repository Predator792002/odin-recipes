Lasagna Recipe

Description

Delight in layers of savory goodness with this classic lasagna recipe. Tender lasagna noodles intermingle with rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and a hearty filling of seasoned ground beef or sausage.

Topped with a blanket of melted cheese, each slice of this comforting Italian dish is a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave you longing for seconds. Dive into the luscious layers of pasta, each bite revealing a harmony of robust marinara sauce, velvety béchamel, and savory meat filling. With a generous sprinkling of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, this lasagna is a timeless masterpiece that captures the essence of Italian home cooking.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare Meat Filling:

    • Cook ground beef or Italian sausage with diced onion and minced garlic until browned and cooked through..
    • Stir in tomato sauce and simmer.

  2. Cook Lasagna Noodles:

    • Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.

  3. Prepare Cheese Mixture:

    • Mix ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, beaten eggs, chopped fresh basil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.

  4. Layer Lasagna:

    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Repeat layers of meat sauce, noodles, and cheese until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.

  5. Bake Lasagna:

    • Sprinkle with additional shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
    • Cover with foil and bake, then remove foil and bake until golden and bubbly.

  6. Finish and Serve:

    • Let lasagna cool before slicing and serving.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.

  7. Enjoy the comforting flavors of homemade lasagna.
